Learning: How Mining & Networks Work

What is Mining?

Mining is the process of validating transactions and securing a blockchain by solving cryptographic puzzles. Successful miners earn rewards.

Proof of Work vs Proof of Stake

Proof of Work uses computational work to secure the network; Proof of Stake selects validators by stake size, using much less energy.

Hashrate & Difficulty
